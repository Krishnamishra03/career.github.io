Steps to Restart Your Career

  1. Reflect on your strengths, interests, and values.
  2. Research industries and roles that align with your goals.
  3. Develop new skills through online courses or certifications.
  4. Update your resume and LinkedIn profile to highlight transferable skills.
  5. Network with professionals and attend industry events.
  6. Apply for internships, freelance projects, or entry-level roles to gain experience.
  7. Continuously learn and adapt to industry trends.
